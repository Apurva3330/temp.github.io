Simulation Research Solutions

Create limitless possibilities for your research environment with Realtime’s world class research driving simulators.

Realtime Technologies is focused on the development of simulation software and hardware to assist in automotive research ranging from vehicle dynamics to the impact of distracted driving. Our solutions allow research teams to model vehicles and driving environments in order to conduct analysis and discover the implications of changes to vehicles, technologies, and driver behaviors. Using our simulation technology, evaluation of the impacts of engineering decisions becomes possible. Our goal is to work with design, engineering, and research terms to develop the best possible simulations that allow for comprehensive control of environmental factors, material choices, and other variables that can improve vehicle and driver performance.

If you’re part of a research team and are interested in exploring how simulation can improve your analysis, contact us today. We’re happy to answer any question you might have about how simulation can help teams conduct research that improves vehicle performance and safety.
