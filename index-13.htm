ABOUT GLOBISENS

ALL-IN-ONE SCIENCE TO FULL STEAM CROSS-OVER SOLUTION!

Globisens is an education technology company that delivers an exciting and enriching STEAM learning experience for tomorrows innovators.

After launching the first hand-held mobile science laboratory for K-12 in 2012, Globisens has continued to set the benchmark for innovation. Today the companys portfolio offers a unique blend of STEAM crossover solutions. Globisens incorporates all-in-one mobile science labs with data analysis, collaborative and coding applications, as well as pixel art creative tools.


THE FIRST K-12 MOBILE SCIENCE LAB

The Globisens story began with the award-winning Labdisc, integrating up to 15 wireless sensors in a single device. Turning any classroom into a multi-disciplinary hand-on science Lab, the Labdisc revolutionized science in terms of cost, convenience and portability.

Today, Globisens has more than 100,000 Labdisc devices, in 20 different languages, installed in over 30 countries, all while winning 7 prestigious international Ed Tech awards.

Seamlessly integrating
hands-on science into learning

Wireless, built-in probes and automatic sensor testingand calibration releases teachers from wasted hours in setup and calibration and saves schools substantial investments in traditional laboratory formats. The Labdisc design fully integrates with the latest technology tools penetrating schools from online learning, to Chromebooks, interactive whiteboards and tablets.

Coding, the new literacy

Globisens introduces coding with the Xploris solution. Now students can take the next step and control machines output according to changes in their sensors readings. Integrating scientific investigation with code generation, students can understand and impact the day-to-day technology that drives their world.

Paralleling real sci-tech processes

Globisens solutions inspire and prepare tomorrows scientists and technologists. Performing hands-on experiments, students can grasp abstract science concepts, master the language of technology and learn to collaborate.

Globisens offers a range of accessible and fun exploration tools, together with data analysis and collaborative software. Sharing their results with GlobiCollab collaborative software enables students to finely tune their working processes, expand their knowledge and broaden their scientific inquiry.

An orchestra of STEAM learning

Just one example of this holistic approach to STEAM learning is seen when using the Labdisc to measure the impact of temperature and light in a greenhouse. Students can collaborate with GlobiCollab to analyze their shared results. Based on their data conclusions they can then apply the Exploris to develop code that can adjust the temperature and light output in the greenhouse.
