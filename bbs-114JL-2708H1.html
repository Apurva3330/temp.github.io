New Standards, New Opportunities

The Next Generation Science Standards* (NGSS) are clear:

  • Students need 3-dimensional learning experiences anchored in phenomena and hands-on learning.
  • Teachers need instructional resources that will help them navigate the new standards and provide these experiences.

Building Blocks of Science 3D is an all-inclusive solution for your students and teachers:

  • 30-minute hands-on investigations that fit into your busy day
  • 3-dimensional investigations anchored in real-world phenomena
  • Complete units that provide lab materials and digital and print resources for all your students—no extra purchases

Informational Text for Reading and Writing

Build students’ literacy skills with 18 new literacy titles from the Building Blocks of Science 3D program. Designed to support the science concepts from Building Blocks of Science units, these readers:

  • Address the NGSS science and engineering practices as well as ELA and mathematics standards
  • Offer on- and below-grade-level readability
  • Are available in English and Spanish
  • Have kindergarten titles formatted as Big Books

Building Blocks of Science 3D units are flexible enough to add science literacy and informational text to any science or literacy program. Available in both printed softcover books and e-books.
