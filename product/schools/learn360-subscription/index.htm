About This Product

Take a quick look at the overview video forLearn360:

With an appealing new design, enhanced functionality, and an improved user experience,Learn360 is the ultimate streaming multimedia resource for K12 schoolsthe complete district solution. Teachers, students, and parents can access more than 182,000 media resources on any Internet-enabled device, anytime, anywhere, and engaging content landing pages make it easy to find the wealth of videos, interactives, printables, audio content, and maps and flags. The entire site and all landing pages can be filtered by grade level (all grades, elementary, or middle/high) for targeted browsing.

Learn360s enhanced, state-of-the-art platform makes it a powerful tool for flipped classrooms, blended instruction, project-based learning, and 1:1 environments. Its media resources are provided by more than 560 top educational content providers, and that number is continually growing.Learn360currently serves more than 22million students in 26,900+schools across the United States and Canada.
